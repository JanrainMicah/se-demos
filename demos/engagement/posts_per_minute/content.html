Posts Per Minute

This is a demo for Janrain Enterprise with Posts Per Minute. Add simple near-realtime analytics to your site with a posts per minute counter -- based on a calculated trend -- and/or a posts per minute graph that gives a visual representation of posts per minute in one minute time slices. Perfect for giving your visitors a quick overview of the social activity behind those trending topics or celebrities..

Commenting

Let customers sign in and publish comments on articles or other site content. Comments are fully integrated with social networks, SEO-friendly, responsive for mobile, and include extensive filtering and moderation tools.

Commenting instances can easily be provisioned automatically with the creation of new pages and/or posts.

Try out these features on this example implementation:
  • After signing into site, you are also signed into commenting
  • Like a post
  • Comment on a comment
  • Share comment to Facebook and Twitter (if logged in with that IDP)
  • Dirty word filter prevents offensive posts
  • Comments added on this page also show in Site Activity Feed
  • Comment sort order choices
  • Users can delete their own posts
