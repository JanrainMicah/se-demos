This is a demo of Janrain Enterprise with Ratings and Reviews.

Empower customers to rate and review products or content across your sites. Create social proof by enabling customers to become advocates. Define sub-categories for customers to rate, and sort reviews based on chronology, number of ratings, most liked or most active. Reviews are fully integrated with social networks, SEO-friendly, responsive for mobile, and include extensive filtering and moderation tools.

Ratings and Review instances can easily be provisioned automatically with the creation of new pages and/or products.

Try out these features on this example implementation:

  • After signing into site, you are also signed into ratings and reviews
  • Like a review
  • Comment on a review
  • Share review to Facebook and Twitter (if logged in with that IDP)
  • Dirty word filter prevents offensive posts
  • Reviews added on this page also show in Site Activity Feed
Coming Soon:
  • Review sort order choices
  • Users can delete their own reviews

Moderation Capabilities

In addition to the dirty-word filter, additional filters can be added to modify the automatic moderation behavior. Comment stream can also be pre-moderated, which requires that all comments be approved before appearing in the stream.
